Addressing Your Hesitations

How can I fit this in? I'm already buried under grading and research. +

This training is built with zero fluff specifically for busy academics. The daily lessons are short, bite-sized, and designed to save you time immediately. If you can spare 15 minutes a day, you will easily save hours on your next grading cycle.

I have absolutely no programming experience. Will I get lost? +

Not at all. You don't need a computer science degree to be data-savvy. We bypass the complex jargon and focus entirely on visual, step-by-step application. If you can navigate basic software, you can easily master these workflows.

Why should I switch from SPSS or manual spreadsheets? +

Manual tracking leads to human error and clunky SPSS menus are rigid. Modern data skills make your data pipelines clean, dynamic, and reproducible. Plus, these skills are highly sought after by modern funding bodies and international journals.

Do I need to buy expensive software or licenses? +

Absolutely not. We rely on standard tools you likely already have (like Excel) and powerful, completely free, open-source options (like Python and Power BI Desktop) that won't cost you or your department anything.

Is this just corporate training repurposed for academics? +

No. This was created by an academic, specifically for academic workflows. The examples, datasets, and scenarios we use are based on student performance, institutional datasets, survey research, and journal requirements—not corporate sales metrics.
